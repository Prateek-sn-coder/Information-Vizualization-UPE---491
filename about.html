About The Project

The sales and profit visualization for Exacompta Clairefontaine showcases the performance of the renowned French stationary brand. It reveals the profitability of Clairefontaine, highlighting periods of high or low profit margins. This visualization allows stakeholders to analyze the overall sales and profit trajectory, identify peak seasons or trends, and assess the brand's financial performance over time. It provides valuable insights for decision-making, strategic planning, and identifying opportunities for optimization within the stationary market.

I hope that this website serves as a valuable resource for exploring and understanding the sales trend and profit this business.

Data Source

The data source used for the visualization of Clairefontaine's sales and profit is a structured dataset derived from internal sales and financial records. This dataset contains detailed information such as date, sales, and profit figures, allowing for a comprehensive analysis of trends over time.
The data is reliable, regularly updated, and undergoes validation to ensure accuracy. It may also incorporate additional variables, such as product categories or customer segments, to provide deeper insights. This data source serves as a valuable foundation for generating meaningful insights and facilitating informed decision-making regarding Clairefontaine's sales and profit performance.

The data source: Exacompta Clairefontaine

About the Dataset

The dataset contains 16 columns and more than 4116 rows after the prep work from tableau prep. The prep work included pivoting the columns to rows.

The dataset can be viewed by clicking on the following link: Dataset

The dataset contains the following attributes or parameters:

  • Order ID : unique order id of products
  • Order Date : date of purchase of the products
  • Country : country to which product is exported
  • Segment : either consumer, corporate or home office
  • Ship Mode : economy, priority or immediate
  • Category : furniture or office supplies
  • Product Name : name of the product purchased
  • Cost : cost to the company for the product (in dollars)
  • Sales : selling price to the company (in dollars)
  • Quantity : number of products sold

Tableau Visualization

To visualize and analyze the dataset, I have utilized a powerful suite of data visualization and analytics tools from Tableau. These tools include Tableau Prep, Tableau Desktop, and Tableau Public. Tableau Prep is used for data preparation and cleaning, while Tableau Desktop enables us to create interactive and insightful visualizations. The final visualizations are published and shared using Tableau Public, allowing users to explore and interact with the data online.
