Sales By Category

The Sales by Category analysis focuses on understanding the sales performance of different product categories within a company. This analysis involves examining the revenue generated by each category, identifying top-selling categories, and uncovering trends or patterns that can drive business decisions. By analyzing sales data across categories, businesses can gain insights into customer preferences, product demand, and areas of growth.

The Sales by Category analysis provides valuable insights into product performance and customer preferences. It helps businesses optimize their product portfolio, allocate resources effectively, and tailor their marketing strategies to maximize sales and profitability.
